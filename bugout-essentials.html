10 Bug-Out Bag Essentials

April 22, 2025

When the grid goes down and the world stops pretending, you're going to need more than a smartphone and good intentions. A real bug-out bag isn’t about fantasy—it’s about function. Here’s what you actually need.

  1. Water Filter or Purification Tablets – Because drinking from a muddy ditch is a bad time without one.
  2. Multi-tool – Your all-in-one survival sidekick. Cut. Pry. Open. Repeat.
  3. Firestarter – Lighters fail. Matches get wet. Carry ferro rods or stormproof matches.
  4. Mylar Blanket – Light, cheap, and the difference between shivering or sleeping.
  5. Compact First Aid Kit – Duct tape and hope aren’t a trauma plan.
  6. Portable Food – High-calorie bars or MREs. It’s not a buffet. It’s survival.
  7. Sturdy Knife – Fixed blade. Full tang. No compromises.
  8. Paracord – Tie down tarps, lash gear, or fix busted boots. A prepper’s duct tape.
  9. Change of Clothes – Dry socks can save your soul.
  10. Map & Compass – GPS dies. You won’t—if you know how to navigate old-school.
