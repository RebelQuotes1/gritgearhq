10 Urban Survival Tips

When the city crumbles, comfort turns to chaos. Forget lattes and streaming — here's how to survive when urban convenience collapses into lawless gridlock.

  1. Get Out Fast: Cities trap people like roaches. Have a bug-out plan. Early exit = best exit.
  2. Grey Man Wins: Dress low-key, don’t look rich. Stay unnoticed. Flashy equals dead.
  3. Stay Off Main Roads: Crowds attract attention and bullets. Learn back routes and alleys.
  4. Silent Kills the Noise: No shouting, no music, no light. Silence = survival.
  5. Know Your Landmarks: GPS might fail. Learn buildings, graffiti routes, and sewer access.
  6. Trade Smart: In a barter economy, skills and items > paper money. Razor blades beat Rolexes.
  7. Scavenge at Odd Hours: Move at dawn or 3 a.m. — avoid desperate mobs.
  8. Use Rooftops and Tunnels: Vertical movement avoids ground-level chaos. Think up/down.
  9. Control Your Group: Too big = slow. Too small = vulnerable. 3–5 disciplined people max.
  10. Watch for Traps: Desperate humans do desperate things. Be paranoid. You're probably right.

Concrete jungles aren't made for survivors — but you’re not average, are you?
